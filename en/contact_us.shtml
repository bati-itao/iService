Contact Us

If you have questions related to IT Accessibility, or if you would like to submit your comments/suggestions, send an email to ACE-CEA@hrsdc-rhdcc.gc.ca or call 819 654-1071.

You may also submit your service requests directly to the National ServiceDesk. Once online, you may type ‘accessibility’ in the product list.
